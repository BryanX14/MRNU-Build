About MRNU

Quality Reclaimed Materials for Smarter Renovations

Maple Ridge New & Used Building Materials is a community-driven supplier focused on sustainability, affordability, and quality. Since opening in 2019, we’ve helped homeowners, contractors, and designers find new and reusable building materials that save money and reduce waste.

From doors and windows to cabinets and architectural pieces, our ever-changing inventory offers practical solutions with character—perfect for renovations, restorations, and custom projects of all sizes.
