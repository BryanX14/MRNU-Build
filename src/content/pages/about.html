About MRNU

Quality Building Materials Since 2019

Since opening in October 2019, Maple Ridge New & Used Building Materials has been helping homeowners, contractors, and DIYers find quality new and reclaimed building materials at affordable prices. As a community-focused business, we're committed to providing a cost-effective and sustainable alternative for renovation and building projects throughout the Lower Mainland.

Our expansive property is home to an ever-changing selection of doors, windows, cabinetry, plumbing fixtures, lighting, hardware, lumber, and more. Whether you're tackling a small home improvement project or a large renovation, our knowledgeable team is here to help you find the right materials at the right price.

Save money, reduce waste, and discover quality building materials for your next project. The MRNU Team Serving the Lower Mainland
